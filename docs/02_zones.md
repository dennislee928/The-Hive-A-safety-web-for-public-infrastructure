# Zones

## Z1 — Station Interior
- Bottlenecks: gates, escalators, platforms
- Guidance: PA/signage/templates; app guidance

## Z2 — Train Car
- Constraints: enclosed environment, limited physical egress
- Guidance: in-car messaging; coordination with next station response

## Z3 — Station Perimeter
- Interfaces: bus interchanges, sidewalks, street crossings
- Guidance: routing to alternate entrances/exits and safe congregation points

## Z4 — Other High-Density Areas
- Events and dispersal flows
- Emphasis: decongestion, consistent guidance, multi-agency coordination
